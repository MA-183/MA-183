> [!tip] Halvvinkel formlene

> $$\begin{aligned} \cos^2(t) = \frac{1+\cos(2t) }{2 }   \end{aligned}$$ 
> og
> $$\begin{aligned} \sin^2(t) = \frac{1-\cos(2t) }{2 }   \end{aligned}$$
